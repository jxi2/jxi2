About

Established in 1982, Jxi2 Inc. designs and manufactures the Syncclock line of bus level timing cards, capable of meeting your time reference needs.

Our products include PCIe, VME, AMC, Mini PCIe, and more. All boards have a wide variety of available options such as GPS synchronization, OCXO time bases, and additional outputs like IRIG-B.

Jxi2 Inc. operates out of El Paso, Texas. Syncclock cards can be purchased through our sales partner, Brandywine Communications.
